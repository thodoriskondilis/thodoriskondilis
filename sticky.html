Sticky banner

A sticky ad or sticky banner is a special advertising form. It is a banner advertisement , which appears during the web page call and always remains visible to the Internet user when the page is scaled or scrolled, so that the top banner edge is always at the edge of the browser window. That is why sticky banners are also referred to as scroll banners or scroll ads. An example of sticky ad:
